# Task-Adaptive Photonic Computing with Programmable Metasurfaces (Simulation Study)

## Abstract
We evaluate task-adaptive programmable photonics under non-stationary shifts. Best recovery-cost tradeoff appears at severity=0.4, fraction=0.25, strategy=local (recovery=1.500).

## 1. Introduction
This manuscript targets simulation-first photonic computing design laws rather than single benchmark wins.

## 2. Methods
A fixed trunk with partial programmable gains is tested under severity and programmable-fraction sweeps; electronic/local/global adaptation strategies are compared.

## 3. Results
Recovery-cost Pareto analysis highlights when sparse adaptation is insufficient and global reconfiguration is preferred.

## 4. Claim boundaries
- Supported claims are restricted to the reported simulation setup.
- Hardware/deployment/SOTA claims are not made.
